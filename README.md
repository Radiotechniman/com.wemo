### WeMo app for Homey  
Connect your Belkin WeMo hardware to Homey.

This app supports:
* Belkin WeMo Led Lighting (Requires Belkin WeMo Link)
* Belkin WeMo Insight
* Belkin WeMo Switch
* Belkin WeMo Light Switch
* Belkin WeMo Motion Sensor
